Department of LAW

The Department of LAW at PUGC is a dynamic hub of intellectual exploration and innovation. Our faculty, comprised of seasoned professionals and industry experts, are dedicated to fostering a stimulating learning environment where students can thrive. Through a rigorous curriculum that seamlessly blends theory and practice, we equip our students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the legal industry. Our state-of-the-art facilities and resources provide a conducive atmosphere for research, collaboration, and personal growth. Join us in the LAW program and discover a world of opportunities to explore your passion for justice and shape your future career in law.

Classes Offered

LLB (Hons.)

The LLB (Hons.) program is a 5-year course designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the legal system, its frameworks, and its application to real-world scenarios. The curriculum covers diverse areas of law, including constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, and human rights, ensuring that graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel as legal professionals.
