Classes Offered

BS English Language & Literature

The BS English Language & Literature is a comprehensive 4-year degree spread across 8 semesters. It is designed to offer students a deep understanding of English language, literature, linguistics, and communication skills. The curriculum provides a balance between classical and contemporary works, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills, preparing graduates for a variety of career paths in education, writing, media, and public service.
