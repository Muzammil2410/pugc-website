Classes Offered

BS Commerce

The BS Commerce program is a 4-year degree that spans 8 semesters. It focuses on equipping students with strong foundational knowledge in accounting, finance, marketing, and management. This program prepares students to pursue professional careers in business, commerce, and public sector organizations, blending theory with practical business strategies.
