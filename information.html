Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology is the offshoot of Punjab University College of Information Technology (PUCIT) with the aim to provide a state of the art learning environment to the youth of the nation. The history of PUCIT dates back to 1987 when the Department of Computer Science was established at the University of the Punjab, Lahore. The Department of IT offers BS in Information Technology, BS Computer Science and M.Sc in Information Technology Programs.

Classes Offered

BS in Computer Science (BSCS)

This is a 4-year program divided into 8 semesters, designed to provide a solid foundation in computer science, including courses in algorithms, programming, databases, and software engineering.

BS in Information Technology (BSIT)

Also a 4-year program, spanning 8 semesters, focused on practical IT skills like network administration, software development, and data management to prepare students for the evolving tech landscape.
