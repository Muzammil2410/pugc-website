Classes Offered

BS Business Administration (BBA)

The BS Business Administration is a 4-year program divided into 8 semesters. This degree equips students with in-depth knowledge of business management, entrepreneurship, and leadership. It covers essential topics such as marketing, finance, human resource management, and strategic planning, preparing students for various roles in business environments.
