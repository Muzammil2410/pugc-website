Classes Offered

BBA (Hons.) Banking & Finance

The BBA (Hons.) in Banking & Finance is a 4-year, 8-semester program designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge of financial systems, banking operations, risk management, and investment strategies. The curriculum blends theoretical foundations with practical exposure, preparing students for leadership roles in banking, finance, investment firms, and related fields. Graduates are well-positioned to take on roles in financial analysis, banking management, investment banking, and other related professions.
